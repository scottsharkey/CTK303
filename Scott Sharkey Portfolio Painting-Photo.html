Covid Through A Lens - 2019

I captured these photos for my digital photography class final that focused on the covid 19 pandemic and how it affected my community. Stores with barren shelves, others using lines and other methods to limit the number of people inside at one time. Places once bustling with people are closed, restaurants and food chains offering new low contact ways to get your food, and new drive-through testing sites.
