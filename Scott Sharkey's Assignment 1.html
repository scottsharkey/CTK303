The Number 1 Song in The World on My Birthday was

Maria Maria by Santana and Featuring The Product G & B



Additional Information

  • The song Maria Maria was released on September 14th, 1999, as apart of the American rock band Santana's album called Supernatural.

  • The song managed to hit number 1 in the United States as well as Canada, France, Germany, Hungary, Sweden, and Switzerland.

  • Maria Maira won the Grammy Award for Best Pop Performance by a Duo or Group with Vocals in 2000.
